Detailed Description

A resolved tensor handle.

The underlying bytes may be available directly (non-dedup or IE_WVIEW_DIRECT), or reconstructable via IE_WVIEW_DEDUP + ie_weights_dedup_materialize.

The descriptor pointer refers to an entry in tensor_map_t and remains valid for the lifetime of the index.
