4  * Lightweight CPU topology discovery (sockets and NUMA nodes).
5  *
6  * The implementation prefers Linux sysfs:
7  * - "/sys/devices/system/cpu/cpu<N>/topology/physical_package_id" to determine
8  * the physical socket (package) id per CPU.
9  * - "/sys/devices/system/node/node<N>/cpulist" to determine NUMA node mapping.
10  *
11  * If sysfs is unavailable or incomplete, a safe fallback is used:
12  * - 1 socket, 1 NUMA node, all online CPUs belong to both.
13  *
14  * This header exposes a small, allocation-owning handle and pure getters that
15  * never fail after successful initialization.
16  * ========================================================================== */
