1/* File: engine/include/ie_kernels.h
2 * -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
3 * @file ie_kernels.h
4 * @brief Kernel dispatch points (generic/AVX2) for GEMV and vector ops.
5 *
6 * @details
7 * This header defines the public CPU-kernel API used by the engine.
8 *
9 * Responsibilities:
10 * - Runtime selection of ISA-specific implementations (e.g., AVX2/FMA).
11 * - GEMV entry points for fp32 weights and quantized-activation variants.
12 * - Small vector math helpers used by fused epilogues (e.g., tanh).
13 * - Reference CPU kernels for attention and MLP building blocks.
14 * - Core math building blocks needed by GPT-OSS orchestration (RMSNorm, RoPE).
15 *
16 * Conventions:
17 * - Callers own all buffers; kernels do not retain pointers after returning.
18 * - After initialization, entry points are thread-safe assuming inputs do not alias.
19 */
