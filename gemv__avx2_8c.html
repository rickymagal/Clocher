static __m256 ie_bf16x8_to_f32 ( __m128i  v_bf16)
inlinestatic

Convert 8 BF16 values to FP32.

BF16 is represented as the high 16 bits of an IEEE-754 FP32 value. Conversion is performed by zero-extending to 32 bits and shifting left by 16.
