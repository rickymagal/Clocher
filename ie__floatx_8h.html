Detailed Description

FP32↔BF16/FP16 conversion helpers (vector forms).

All routines are pure and do not allocate. They operate on contiguous arrays. Rounding:

  • FP32→BF16 uses round-to-nearest-even (ties to even).
  • FP32→FP16 uses IEEE 754 round-to-nearest-even; infinities/NaNs preserved.
